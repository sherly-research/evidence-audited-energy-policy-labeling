# Evidence-Audited NLP Pipeline for Indonesian Energy and Renewable Energy Regulations

This repository provides the output package for an evidence-audited NLP pipeline for clause-level policy theme labeling of Indonesian energy and renewable energy regulations.

The pipeline transforms legal regulatory texts into traceable clause-level units, separates appendices, filters non-substantive clauses, assigns weighted rule-assisted policy themes, records evidence audit fields, and evaluates silver-label learnability using TF-IDF-based classical baselines.

## Corpus

The output package was generated from five Indonesian energy and renewable energy regulations:

1. Law No. 30/2007 on Energy
2. Government Regulation No. 79/2014 on National Energy Policy
3. Presidential Regulation No. 22/2017 on the National Energy General Plan
4. Presidential Regulation No. 112/2022 on the Acceleration of Renewable Energy Development for Electricity Supply
5. Law No. 21/2014 on Geothermal Energy

## Main Results

The final pipeline produced:

- 1,027 raw clause-level units
- 1,005 substantive clauses
- 747 clauses labeled into six policy themes
- 258 clauses retained as `Other/Needs Review`
- 221 geothermal-related clauses
- 112 renewable-power-related clauses
- 77 tariff/procurement-related clauses
- 19 fossil-transition-related clauses

The best silver-label learnability model was TF-IDF + Linear SVM, with a stratified cross-validation macro F1 score of 0.8211.
